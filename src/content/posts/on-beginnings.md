To begin again is not to erase what came before. It's to finally see it clearly.

There's something about standing at the edge of a new chapter that makes everything feel sharper. The air is different. The questions are different. You stop asking "what went wrong" and start asking "what comes next."

![Mountains at dawn](/images/beginnings.jpg)

I think beginnings deserve more credit than we give them. We celebrate endings — graduations, wrap parties, last days. But the moment you decide to start? That's where the real courage lives.
